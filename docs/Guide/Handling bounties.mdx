### ::freeze

âœ¨
Freeze the bounty clock. The due date will be adjusted once the `!::unfreeze` is executed.
The bounty manager can use this while reviewing the works without decreasing the duration available for the assignee.

#### Requeriments:

- Sender: Manager,
- Status: In progress,
- Frozen: False

### ::unfreeze
